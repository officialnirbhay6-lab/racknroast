Rack n Roast, where gathering comes naturally

Guided by play, taste, and community. A destination split across two curated floors: a premium gaming lounge featuring professional snooker, pool, and PS5 gaming on the 4th floor, and a gorgeous open-air rooftop restaurant on the 5th floor.

The word **"Rack"** represents our dedication to the cue: the meticulous alignment of snooker and pool balls. The word **"Roast"** pays tribute to our 100% pure vegetarian kitchen: fresh wood-fired pizzas, sizzling appetizers, and hand-roasted coffees.

Around our tables and lounges, play and dining come together. A space designed to connect friends, spark competitive play, and capture moments that linger.
